- Never dominant in field of view

- Floating panels

## SPATIAL INTERACTION RULES

### ‚úÖ ALLOWED
- Look, point, select
- Move panels
- Resize workspace
- Navigate context spatially

### ‚ùå FORBIDDEN
- Gesture-only confirmations
- Implicit consent
- Automatic execution
- Hidden interactions

**All confirmations must remain explicit and readable.**

---

## XR MEETINGS (SPATIAL COLLABORATION)

XR meetings are shared workspaces, not virtual rooms.

### Default State
- No avatars required
- No spatial audio by default
- No recording
- No AI assistance

### Optional
- Minimal avatars (upper-body or abstract)
- Shared spatial documents
- Pointer indicators

**AI behavior rules remain identical to 2D meetings.**

---

## AVATARS IN XR

> Avatars are representations, not identities.

### Rules
- ‚ùå No emotional exaggeration
- ‚ùå No dominance gestures
- ‚ùå No forced eye contact
- ‚ùå No behavioral persuasion

### Nova Avatar in XR
- Optional
- Smaller scale
- Reduced animation
- Governance-only presence

---

## XR + VOICE

- Voice requires same consent rules
- Push-to-talk preferred
- Always-visible listening indicator
- Voice never implies execution

**Voice + XR must remain predictable.**
